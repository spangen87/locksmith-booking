About Us

Best Lås & Säkerhetssystem AB has operated in Sweden for almost 45 years, a company characterized by accuracy and absolute affection towards its customers. "BEST" SWEDEN was founded by Hans Jarkell back in 1974, the company was later taken over by Hans Edling in 1989 and today the company is owned and run by first and second generation family members. Family feeling and responsibility have always characterized our business, we are a very personal company that creates personal contact with all our customers.

Best Lås started as a pure distributor of BEST ACCESS SYSTEM, but has today developed into a complete security supplier. Today, we do both installations and are resellers for several suppliers.

Our quality and our flexibility, which gives us a unique ability to adapt to customers, are our greatest strengths and today we deliver everything from the slightly simpler standard locking system to security systems of a very complex nature. We don't just see ourselves as a security provider, we create together with our customers their own unique security systems.

Welcome to a proud and stable family business where today's more than 40-year-old watchwords are still very much alive - reliability, efficiency and quality.
